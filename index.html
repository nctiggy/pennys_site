Puppy essentials:

Puppy food

It is smart to have good puppy food that your dog can enjoy and will help them grow into a strong healthy dog. Make sure to keep the food consistant and find food that you can trust.

Treats

Having treats for your pup is smart esspecially when you are training, treats can be used as a reward for good behavor.

Food and water bowls

Having food and a water bowl can help you remember to always keep their water bowl full and to feed them.

Collar, harness, leash, ID tag

It is important that you walk your puppy multiple times a day. Having a leash, harness, collar, and ID tag will make it possible to walk your dog safely.

Crate and dog bed

If you work but dont trust your puppy alone at the house able to roam free crate your pup while your gone. Make sure you have a big enough crate to fit your pup and a comfy bed to keep it warm.

Cleaning products

You will want to have cleaning products just incase your puppy has an accident.

Toys

Puppys and dogs always love a good toy to play with and like it even more if they get to pick it out! Take your dog to a pet store so they can pick out a toy that thy will love.

Shampoo and Grooming tools

Even if you plan to take your puppy to grooming appointments, you should have basic grooming products at home. Dog-friendly shampoo, a comb, a brush, nail clippers are essential tools to have.

A gate

Want to keep your puppy out of spacific rooms get a gate to block your pup from getting into them.

Portible water bottles

This is more for people who like to take long hikes. To keep your dog hidrated get a good doggy water bottle.

Training your puppy:

To set yourself up for sucess when training your puppy it is smart to make sure that you have a plan for what you are going to be training your puppy and to have treats near you to reward them along the way. It is smart to start training your puppy from day one so they can strat learning right away what not to do so they dont keep the bad habits when they are fully grown dogs. Make sure to reward good behavior, it will help your puppy know when they are being good. Also make sure to not incourage the bad behavior, when your puppy does something that you dont want them doing use a stern voice and say NO! Never yell at your puppy while training it might scare them or make it so they dont want to listen to you. Look for signs that your pup might need to go potty.


TIP: Try putting a bell on your door and train your furry friend to ring it when he/she needs to go to the bathroom, that way your dog will have less acceidents because you are more aware that they need to go.
